Contact

I am deluged with incoming messages on multiple channels! So,

  • I don't obsessively check electronic messaging services,
  • I often ignore electronic messages so that I can stay focused on whatever I'm doing,
  • on a daily basis, I only interact with email and texts, and even then, I cannot keep up and leave many messages unread, and finally
  • if you want to use something else, Whatsapp is the only other option, and I rarely look at it and have all notifications turned off.

So, there's a good chance I will miss or triage your message!

If you really want to get my attention, text me, or better yet, call me.
